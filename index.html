DayZRP Zen Skills Planner Guide


Select a Skill Tree
Use the icons at the top to switch between Survival, Hunting, Gathering, and Crafting.

Add or Remove Perk Ranks
Use the + and - buttons on each perk card to invest ranks.

Unlock Higher Tiers
Investing ranks in earlier tiers unlocks higher tiers.

Perk Card States
Perks appear in different visual states depending on their availability:

Dimmed – The perk is locked and cannot be selected yet.
Normal – The perk is available and can be upgraded.
Highlighted – The perk currently has ranks invested in it.

Highlighted Skill Tree Tabs
Skill trees that have points invested will display a faint colored highlight around their tab.

Track Your Build
The panels on the right show ranks invested and EXP required.

Copy Build to Clipboard
Copies a readable version of your build for sharing.

Share My Build
Generates a link that loads your exact build when opened.
